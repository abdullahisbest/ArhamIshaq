About Arham: Exploring Life as a MBBS Student at SZMC RYK


Welcome to Arham's world of vlogging! Arham, a dedicated 2nd-year MBBS student at SZMC RYK, is your guide to the thrilling journey of medical school in Pakistan. Through his lens, you'll get an inside look at the life of a medical student, a bookworm, and a passionate vlogger.

Meet Arham


Arham, hailing from the vibrant land of Pakistan, embarked on his academic journey with one clear goal - to become a skilled medical professional. He is currently pursuing his Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree at Sheikh Zayed Medical College, Rahim Yar Khan. His dedication to the field of medicine is unwavering, and he's eager to share the highs, lows, and everything in between with his viewers.


The MBBS Adventure


Arham's vlogs offer a unique peek into the life of a medical student in Pakistan. He'll take you through the rigorous academic challenges, the fascinating world of anatomy and physiology, and the emotional ups and downs of patient interactions. His in-depth knowledge and passion for medicine shine through every video, making complex medical topics more accessible to all.


A Bookworm's Paradise


Arham isn't just a medical student; he's also a fervent book enthusiast. When he's not in the lecture hall or the hospital, you'll find him lost in the world of books. He's a dedicated "bookwitter," sharing his thoughts on the latest literary gems, offering recommendations, and even hosting book reviews on his channel. Join him in exploring the power of words and storytelling.


Writing the Book of Life


Arham believes that life is the greatest book of all, and each experience is a chapter waiting to be written. His vlogs capture not only the academic side of his journey but also the moments of laughter, friendship, and personal growth that define his life as a student. With Arham, you'll see how every day is an opportunity to write a new page in your own book of life.


Join the Journey


Are you curious about the life of a medical student in Pakistan? Do you share Arham's love for books and storytelling? Are you ready to embark on a journey through the world of medicine and beyond? If the answer is yes, then subscribe to Arham's channel and follow him on social media. Stay updated with his latest vlogs, book recommendations, and personal insights.


Arham invites you to be a part of his incredible journey. Let's explore the world of medicine, literature, and life together. Welcome to Arham's vlogging world - where every moment is a lesson and every experience is a story waiting to be told.
